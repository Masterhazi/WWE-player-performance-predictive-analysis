January 27, 2024 | Tampa Bay, Florida

WWE Royal Rumble 2024

Details
Match Card & Results | WWE PPV

WWE Royal Rumble 2024 was a Pay Per View event produced by World Wrestling Entertainment, which took place on January 27, 2024 , at the Tropicana Field in Tampa Bay, Florida.

It's the 37° edition of the Royal Rumble event series, after WWE Royal Rumble 2023.

The show features wrestlers from the Raw and SmackDown brands.

The event had an attendance of 48,044 fans. The main event of the show was 30-Man Royal Rumble match.

Below you can find the full match card and results of WWE Royal Rumble 2024, and more information about the event.
